## 7. ADR-001: Business Model Selection

### Decision
Selected SaaS subscription model with tiered pricing and transaction fees.

### Context
- Need recurring revenue for sustainability
- Must accommodate various customer sizes
- Transaction fees align with usage

### Consequences
+ Predictable revenue stream
+ Scalable with customer growth
- Higher initial development cost
- Complex pricing management

### Status
Accepted
